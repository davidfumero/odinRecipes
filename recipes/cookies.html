Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A gluten free, chewy, chocolate chip cookie made from oat flour, a bit of casein protein powder, and pureed sweet potatoes. These cookies are low calorie and have great flavor for a cookie that has no butter or sugar.

Ingredients

  • 4.17g oat flour
  • 1.67g tapioca flour
  • 1.33g vanilla casein protein powder
  • 11.67g mashed sweet potatoes
  • 4.17g liquid egg whites
  • 3.33g mini chocolate chips
  • 0.17ml vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Mix the oat flour, tapioca flour, protein powder together until it is fully incorporated.
  3. Add in the remaining ingredients and mix until it forms a dough.
  4. Spray a sheet pan with oil and use a cookie scoop to form each cookie. It will make 12.
  5. Wet your fingers and press each cookie into shape, they will not spread in the oven.
  6. Bake for 6-7 minutes. Let the cookies cool before serving.
