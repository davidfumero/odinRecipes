Firecracker Beef and Brussels

Description

Firecracker Beef and Brussels sprouts served with a side of rice. The firecracker sauce is made using a combination of red pepper flakes, hot sauce, and honey for a spicy, sweet, and flavor packed bite.

This recipe makes 5 servings. one serving is 620 calories with 63g of carbs, 44g of protein, and 21g of fat.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups old fashioned oats
  • 2 scoops vanilla whey protein powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 4 medium apples
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups skim milk
  • 5 tbsp vanilla greek yogurt

Steps

For the apples
  1. Wash the apples. Cut them in half and remove the cores. Cut them into thin slices.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add 1 tbsp of butter and then add the apples.
  3. Sprinkle the cinnamon and brown sugar over the top. Cook until the sugar has reduced and the apples have become soft.
For the oatmeal bake
  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, mis together the oats, protein powder, and baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and 1 tbsp of melted butter. Pour over the top of apples and oats and mix.
  4. Spray a 13x9 pan with oil and add the oat mixture and apples.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
Plating
  1. This recipe makes 6 servings. Cut the oatmeal into six pieces. Use the vanilla greek yogurt as a frosting. Enjoy!
