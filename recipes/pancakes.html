Protein Pancakes

Description

Protein pancakes made from oat flour and a blend of whey and casein protein for a high protein breakfast option. You can prep the dry ingredients in advanced and have these ready to go quickly each morning.

Ingredients

  • 6.25g vanilla whey/casein blend 50/50 blend. I mix 12.5g of whey and 12.5g of casein together
  • 5g oat flour
  • 2.5g tapioca flour or cornstarch
  • 0.25g baking powder
  • 15g liquid egg whites
  • 18.75g plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 6.25g water

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients together so no clumps are present. NOTE: I have tested this with only whey protein and it doesn't turn out as well. Only casein will work but you may need more water in the batter. The 50/50 blend is best.
  2. Add in the wet ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  3. Heat a griddle/skillet on medium heat and spray with oil. Add the batter and cook for a couple minutes, then flip.
